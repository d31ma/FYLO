Download FYLO

FYLO ships as a single self-contained binary — no runtime to install. Grab the build for your platform, then use it from 13 languages: a thin client for Python, Ruby, Node/TypeScript, PHP, Go, Rust, C#, Java, or Dart, plus local-first browser, mobile (iOS/Android), and Flutter clients — all in the version-matched fylo-clients bundle attached to each release.

Install (macOS & Linux)

Detects your OS and architecture, verifies the checksum, and puts fylo on your PATH. 

{unixInstall()}

Install (Windows)

Run in PowerShell — installs under %LOCALAPPDATA%\Fylo and updates your user PATH. 

{windowsInstall()}
